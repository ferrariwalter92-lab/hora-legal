Account Deletion Request

App Name: HORA

HORA allows users to request the deletion of their account and associated data at any time.

How to delete your account

  • Open the HORA app
  • Go to Settings
  • Tap on "Delete Account"
  • Confirm your request

Once confirmed, your account will be permanently deleted.

Alternatively, you can request account deletion by contacting:
support@hora.app

Data that will be deleted

  • Phone number and account information
  • Username and profile data
  • Connections with other users
  • Activity status and availability data

Data retention

Some data may be temporarily retained for up to 30 days for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance purposes. After this period, all data is permanently deleted from our systems.
