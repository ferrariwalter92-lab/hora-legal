Child Safety & CSAE Policy

HORA is committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users and has a zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse and exploitation.

1. Zero-Tolerance Policy

HORA strictly prohibits any content, behavior, or interaction involving the sexual abuse, exploitation, or endangerment of minors.

This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Grooming or attempted grooming of minors
  • Any sexualized, exploitative, abusive, or otherwise harmful conduct involving individuals under the age of 18

Any violation of this policy may result in immediate account suspension or permanent removal and may be reported to relevant authorities where required by law.

2. Reporting and Review

Users may report suspected violations of this policy by contacting HORA through the contact information provided below.

Reports related to child safety are reviewed promptly, and appropriate action is taken based on the nature and severity of the issue.

3. Enforcement

HORA reserves the right to investigate reports, remove offending accounts or content, and take any action necessary to help protect users and comply with applicable laws and platform requirements.

4. Legal Compliance

HORA complies with applicable child protection laws and cooperates with law enforcement and relevant authorities where legally required.

5. Contact

For concerns, reports, or inquiries related to child safety, please contact:
